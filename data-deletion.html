Request Data Deletion Within the App (Primary Method)

AfterVeil provides an in-app data deletion mechanism, in accordance with Apple App Store requirements.

To request deletion within the app:

  1. Open AfterVeil on your device
    Launch the game on your device
  2. Access Game Settings
    From the Story Selection screen (main screen with available cases), tap the "Game Settings" button at the bottom of the screen
  3. Navigate to Data & Privacy
    In Game Settings, scroll down to the "Data & Privacy" section
  4. Initiate Deletion
    Tap "Delete All Data" (red button with trash icon)
  5. Review the Confirmation
    Review the confirmation dialog, which explains that this will permanently delete your User ID, Installation ID, all game progress, purchased cases, settings, downloaded content, and data from both your device and our servers.
  6. Confirm Deletion
    Tap "Delete Everything" to confirm, or "Cancel" to abort
  7. Wait for Completion
    Wait for the deletion process to complete. You will see a confirmation message showing what was deleted. The app will automatically close after successful deletion.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your data deletion request is completed, we will delete the following data from our systems:

  • Your User ID and Installation ID
  • All game progress and saves
  • Purchased cases and items
  • Settings and preferences
  • All downloaded content
  • Data from both your device and our servers
  • Account profile and authentication data
  • In-game chat messages, notes, and user-generated content
  • Feedback, reports, and support communications
  • Any other personal data associated with your AfterVeil account

Data We Cannot Delete

Certain information may be retained by third parties in accordance with their own legal obligations:

  • Records of in-app purchases are managed by Apple App Store or Google Play
  • We do not control or delete data stored by app store providers
  • Refunds, billing history, and transaction records are subject to the app store's policies
